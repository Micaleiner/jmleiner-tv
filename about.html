The Anthology

Five stand-alone psychological suspense stories about deception, power, survival, and one woman's refusal to stop reading what others hope will remain hidden.

The worlds are fictional. The understanding of how systems conceal harm is not.

A Free Spirit Who Learned to Survive

Mica is by nature a free spirit—untamed, instinctive, refusing compromise. But three decades inside institutional systems demanded something different. She learned to think in ten-year horizons. She learned strategy, control, calculated patience. She learned to read the systems from inside them without being consumed by them.

She lost touch with her freedom. She didn't lose touch with herself.

But the wild horse never stops saddling up. Periodically, throughout her life, she breaks the pattern. She refuses the safe choice. She tells the truth that costs her. She walks out with the evidence. This breaking free, this refusal to stay controlled—it's expressed through her art, her creative work, and ultimately through The Deception Anthology itself. The series is her saddling the wild horse again.

How knowledge becomes story.

The Authority Behind the Work

Mica brings decades of professional experience across multiple continents—corporate finance, technology, advertising, creative industries, fashion design, event management, and hospitality. She understands institutional architecture from the inside: how systems conceal harm, how procedures protect themselves above all else.

Season One

Fashion Design &
Entrepreneurship

The boutique at Savile & Sage is built from lived knowledge of design, craft, luxury, and the people who commission beauty. Every detail reflects reality: reputation, clientele, and the way aesthetic excellence becomes a hiding place for something darker.

Seasons Two & Three

Finance &
Legal Systems

Corporate fraud revealed through the trained eye that spots variances. Insider trading exposed through an understanding of how legal systems protect themselves. Financial and legal literacy become tools for reading institutional architecture.

Season Four

Hospitality &
General Management

After decades navigating corporate and legal systems, Mica takes the guesthouse—a concrete thing she can control. But in Coolum, she discovers local government corruption operates by different rules.
