Zugzwang

Advertising  ·  Corporate architecture  ·  1991–1999

Advertising as the institutional frame. Abigail enters a corporate architecture where value is moved inside the group to where it is most needed. The danger is not theft in the obvious sense. It is a structure that makes every move look authorised.
